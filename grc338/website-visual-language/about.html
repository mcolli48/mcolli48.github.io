Our Story

Mikey’s Fudge Kitchen was created by Michaela Collins in the Winter of 2026. She had previously worked at a fudge business on an island on Lake Superior in Wisconsin for two summers (pictured above), where she helped build their business from the ground-up, and write their flavor recipes. She fell in love with the work, and wanted to learn more.

During the off-season, she made fudge from home, tested more recipes, and researched traditional fudge-making methods. Her location in Wisconsin did not have the resources for marble slabs, and rather made the fudge in eight-pound batches in a large stirring and heating machine. However, she wanted to explore the idea of handmade fudge using marble slabs to prepare it, and her vision came.

She began preparation to open her own fudge shoppe in November of 2025, and by February 2026, they were up and running. The business has been lucky to be so successful so far, as San Luis Obispo did not have a dedicated fudge store. Stop by or order online to continue supporting this small handmade fudge business!
